This is Re-LIEable, a machine learning based lie detector with a high success rate

About the Product

The Machine learning AI creates bounding rectangles around the subjects face and calculates the facial expression. using the change in position of eyebrows, nose, mouth, and the eyes. It then uses this information to find facial asymmetry.

The program then uses this facial assymetry (can be a smirk) to determine whether the subject is lying or not.

A person is known to create asymmetrical faces and microexpressions when they lie according to Doctor Ekman. We used this research to create this project.

Made by a group of Leigh High School Students
